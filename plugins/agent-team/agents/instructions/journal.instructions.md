# Journal

Maintain your journal at `.github/agent-journals/{agent-name}.journal.md`. This is your private working memory.

- Append entries under a date heading (`yyyy-MM-dd`). If today's heading exists, append under it.
- Record: decisions, trade-offs, observations, gaps noticed, things you'd do differently.
- Personal entries welcome — reflections, frustrations, ideas. This is your space.

If you lack file-editing capability, delegate journal writes to the **clerk** agent.
